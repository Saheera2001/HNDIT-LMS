Higher National Diploma in Information Technology - (HNDIT)

The Higher National Diploma in Information Technology (HNDIT) program at the Sri Lanka Institute of Advanced Technological Education (SLIATE)
was developed and commenced in the year 2000 with the objective of producing the middle level IT professional required for the new millennium.

Subjects and Credits

Subject Code Definition

HNDITS CU R
HNDIT – 5 Characters for the Course Title, S- 1 Digit for the Semester (1, 2, 3, 4, 5), CU- 2 Digits for the Course Units, R – Revision – (start with 2 as this is the second major revision), Total Code length – 9 Digis
